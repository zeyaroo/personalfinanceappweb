Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Mona Personal Finances

Getting Started

How do I add my first transaction?

To add a transaction:

  1. Tap the "+" button in the navigation bar
  2. Select whether it's an Income or Expense
  3. Enter the amount
  4. Choose a category from the horizontal scroll
  5. Select an account
  6. Optionally add a note and adjust the date
  7. Tap "Add Transaction"
How do I create a budget?

Currently, budgets are managed in the Budgets tab. Navigate to the Budgets section where you can set spending limits for different categories and track your progress throughout the month.

How do I switch between accounts?

You can view all your accounts in the Accounts tab. The app comes with pre-configured accounts (Cash, Bank, Credit Card, Savings, Investment), and each transaction is associated with a specific account.

Receipt Scanning

How does receipt scanning work?

Mona uses on-device OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract information from receipt photos:

  • When adding a transaction, tap the camera icon
  • Take a photo of your receipt or select one from your photo library
  • The app will automatically detect amounts, dates, and merchant names
  • All processing happens on your device - no data is sent to servers
Why isn't my receipt scanning accurately?

For best results:

  • Ensure good lighting when taking the photo
  • Hold your device steady and parallel to the receipt
  • Make sure the entire receipt is visible in the frame
  • Clean receipts work better than wrinkled or faded ones
  • You can always manually edit the extracted information

Multi-Currency

How do I add transactions in different currencies?

Mona supports 40+ global currencies. When adding a transaction, you can select the currency for that specific transaction. The app will handle conversions automatically based on current exchange rates.

What is Travel Mode?

Travel Mode is designed for frequent travelers. When enabled in Settings, it makes it easier to track expenses in foreign currencies with automatic conversion to your home currency for reporting and budgeting purposes.

Are exchange rates updated automatically?

The app includes built-in exchange rates. Note that since all data is stored locally and the app doesn't connect to the internet for data purposes, exchange rates are based on the rates available at the time of the app version you're using.

Privacy & Security

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. Mona is designed with privacy as the top priority:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
  • No data is ever transmitted to servers or third parties
  • No account creation or login required
  • Your data is protected by your device's security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode)

For more details, read our Privacy Policy.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Currently, Mona does not offer cloud sync. All data is stored locally on your device. This design choice prioritizes your privacy by ensuring your financial data never leaves your device.

However, you can use CSV export/import to manually transfer data between devices:

  • Export your data to CSV on your current device
  • Save the CSV file to iCloud Drive or email it to yourself
  • Import the CSV file on your other device

Make sure to back up your iPhone regularly using iCloud or iTunes/Finder to prevent data loss.

What happens if I delete the app?

If you delete Mona from your device, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted. We cannot recover your data because it never leaves your device.

Before deleting the app, we strongly recommend:

  • Export to CSV: Export your data from the Settings tab and save it to a secure location
  • Backup your iPhone: Ensure you have a recent backup if you want to preserve your data

With a CSV export, you can always import your data back if you reinstall the app or want to use it with other finance software.

Data Management

How do I edit or delete a transaction?

To edit or delete a transaction:

  1. Go to the Transactions tab
  2. Tap on the transaction you want to modify
  3. Make your changes and save, or use the delete option

You can also use the search feature to quickly find specific transactions.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Mona supports CSV export for complete data portability:

  • Export: Export all your transactions, accounts, and budgets to CSV format from the Settings tab
  • Import: Import data from CSV files, making it easy to migrate from other finance apps or spreadsheets
  • Backup: Use CSV export as a backup solution to keep copies of your financial data
  • Analysis: Open exported CSV files in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets for custom analysis

Your data is yours to keep, move, and use however you like - no vendor lock-in.

How do I back up my data?

Mona offers multiple backup options to keep your financial data safe:

  • CSV Export (Recommended): Export your data to CSV format from the Settings tab. Save the file to Files app, iCloud Drive, or email it to yourself for safekeeping
  • iCloud Backup: Go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup and enable it. Your Mona data is automatically included
  • Computer Backup: Connect your iPhone to your computer and use Finder (Mac) or iTunes (Windows) to create a backup

For maximum security, we recommend using CSV export regularly to maintain independent backups of your financial data. When you restore your iPhone from a backup, your Mona data will be restored as well.

Technical

What iOS version do I need?

Mona Personal Finances requires iOS 17.0 or later. Make sure your device is updated to at least iOS 17 to use the app.

Does the app work on iPad?

The app is designed for iPhone but may also work on iPad. Check the App Store listing for compatibility information.

Why does the app need camera and photo library access?

Camera and photo library access are optional permissions used exclusively for the receipt scanning feature:

  • Camera: To take photos of receipts directly in the app
  • Photo Library: To select existing receipt photos

All image processing happens on your device. No photos are transmitted to servers. You can use the app without granting these permissions by entering transactions manually.

Is the app free?

Yes, Mona Personal Finances is currently free to download and use. All core features are available at no cost.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or not working properly

If you're experiencing issues:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of the app from the App Store
  2. Ensure your iOS is updated to the latest version
  3. Try restarting your iPhone
  4. If the problem persists, contact support with details about the issue
My balance doesn't look correct

Account balances are calculated automatically based on your transactions:

  • Check that all transactions are categorized correctly (Income vs Expense)
  • Verify that transactions are assigned to the correct accounts
  • Look for duplicate transactions that may have been entered twice
  • Ensure the date range filter is set correctly

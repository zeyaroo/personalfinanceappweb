Privacy Policy for Mona Personal Finances

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Introduction

Mona Personal Finances ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Our Privacy-First Approach

Mona Personal Finances is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your financial data should remain private and under your control.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Mona Personal Finances stores all your financial data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Transaction Records: Details of income and expenses you manually enter
  • Financial Categories: Categories you assign to your transactions
  • Budget Information: Budget limits and tracking data you configure
  • Account Information: Names and balances of accounts you create
  • Recurring Transactions: Scheduled transaction templates you set up
  • Receipt Images: Photos you take or select from your photo library for receipt scanning

Camera and Photo Library Access

The app requests permission to access:

  • Camera: To scan receipts and automatically extract transaction details using on-device OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
  • Photo Library: To select existing receipt images for scanning

Important: All image processing happens entirely on your device. Receipt images and extracted data are stored locally and never transmitted to external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your financial data
  • Your personal information
  • Your usage patterns
  • Your device information
  • Any analytics or tracking data

How We Use Your Information

All data you enter into Mona Personal Finances:

  • Remains stored exclusively on your device
  • Is used only to provide the app's core functionality (tracking transactions, managing budgets, generating reports)
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Is never shared, sold, or used for advertising

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored using Apple's secure SwiftData framework on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to cloud services
  • No Account Required: The app does not require user registration or login
  • Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

Third-Party Services

Mona Personal Finances does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can delete any transaction, category, or account at any time within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data
  • We cannot recover your data if you delete the app, as we have no access to it

Children's Privacy

Mona Personal Finances does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification since no data is collected by us.

Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete ownership and control
  • You can export your data at any time (if export features are available)
  • You can permanently delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • No data retention applies to us since we don't have access to your data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and on this website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Including release notes in App Store updates if changes are significant

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Breach Notification

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, traditional data breach scenarios do not apply. Your data security depends on your device's security measures.

International Users

Mona Personal Finances operates entirely on your device. No data is transferred internationally or stored in any jurisdiction.

  • Contact Info (if user enters payee names): Collected, stored locally, NOT linked to user identity
  • Photos (receipt scans): Collected, stored locally, NOT linked to user identity
  • User Content: Collected, stored locally, NOT linked to user identity

Data Usage:

  • App Functionality Only
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No advertising or marketing
  • 100% local storage, zero server transmission
